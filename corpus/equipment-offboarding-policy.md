# Equipment and Offboarding Policy

## Equipment Issuance

New employees are issued a laptop, monitor, and peripherals within their
first 3 business days, tracked by asset tag in the IT inventory system.
Employees may request a laptop upgrade every 3 years or sooner with
manager approval if the device no longer meets performance needs.

## Resignation Notice

Employees are asked to provide at least 2 weeks of written notice before
their last day. Employees in engineering leadership roles are asked to
provide 4 weeks of notice to support knowledge transfer and hiring
continuity.

## Offboarding Checklist

On an employee's last day, IT must revoke VPN, email, and application
access by 5:00 PM, and HR must conduct an exit interview. Managers are
responsible for collecting all company equipment, including laptops,
badges, and any company-owned mobile devices, before the employee departs.

## Equipment Return

Departing employees must return all company equipment within 5 business
days of their last day, using a prepaid shipping label provided by IT for
remote employees. Equipment not returned within 30 days is charged to the
employee at replacement cost, deducted from final pay where permitted by
law.

## Final Pay and Benefits Continuation

Final paychecks, including accrued PTO payout, are issued according to
state law, typically within the next regular pay cycle. Departing
employees receive COBRA continuation information for health benefits
within 14 days of their termination date.
