# Business Travel Policy

## Booking Process

All business travel must be booked through the Concur travel portal at
least 7 business days before departure for domestic trips and 14 business
days for international trips. Exceptions for urgent travel require
director approval and must be documented in the booking notes.

## Airfare Class

Economy class is standard for flights under 6 hours. For flights over 6
hours, employees at director level and above may book Premium Economy.
Business class requires VP approval regardless of flight duration.

## Lodging Limits

Hotel spend is capped at $250/night for domestic travel in standard
cities and $350/night in designated high-cost cities (New York, San
Francisco, London, Tokyo). Bookings above the cap require pre-approval
from the traveler's manager.

## Ground Transportation

Rideshare and taxi expenses are reimbursable for airport transfers and
client meetings. Rental cars require a mid-size or smaller vehicle unless
transporting equipment or more than 3 colleagues, in which case an SUV may
be booked.

## International Travel Requirements

Employees traveling internationally must register their trip with the
Security team at least 5 business days in advance and confirm passport
validity of at least 6 months beyond the return date. The company
provides emergency travel assistance coverage through a third-party
provider for all approved international trips.
