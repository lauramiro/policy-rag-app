# Remote Work Policy

## Eligibility

Employees are eligible for remote work after completing 6 months of
continuous employment and receiving written manager approval. Roles
classified as "on-site required" (facilities, front-desk reception, and
lab technicians) are not eligible regardless of tenure.

## Work Location and Hours

Remote employees must designate a primary work location on file with HR
and update it within 5 business days of any change. Core collaboration
hours are 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM in the employee's local time zone; outside
of core hours, employees may flex their schedule as long as they complete
40 hours per week.

## Home Office Stipend

The company provides a one-time home office setup stipend of $500,
reimbursable through the expense system within the first 60 days of
approved remote status. An additional $30/month stipend is provided for
internet and utilities, paid via payroll.

## Equipment and Security

Remote employees must use company-issued laptops with full-disk
encryption enabled and must connect to internal systems only via the
company VPN. Use of personal devices to access production systems is
prohibited. Home Wi-Fi networks must use WPA2 or WPA3 encryption.

## In-Office Expectations

Employees on a hybrid arrangement must be in the office a minimum of 2
days per week, coordinated with their team lead. Fully remote employees
are expected to attend in-person all-hands events quarterly, with travel
expenses covered per the Travel Policy.
